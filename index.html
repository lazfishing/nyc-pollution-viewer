About the NYC Pollution Viewer
Understanding our community pollution levels gives us insight into the air we breathe everyday.

The NYC Pollution Viewer provides five years of pollution data from the Environment and Health Data Portal, for Black Carbon, Nitric Oxide and Fine Particulate Matter at the community district level.

Mapping your community pollution levels


1. Select the pollutant you want to view



2. View the pollution levels across years by dragging the slider
